About the Project

The project I am showcasing on this website is the final project for the class Typography and Iconography that I took last semester (fall of 2018). This project was to create a UI kit and digital branding guidelines for a company of our choice. The company I chose for the project was Alienware. The UI kit I made includes 5 icons, buttons on white and black background, buttons when hovered over, a form kit and more. The digitial branding guidelines was to show the do's and dont's for when desinging digtially for Alienware.
